Walking Talking

❮ ❯

"WalkingTalking", a serious mobile game designed for children with articulation speech disorder. Developed using Unity3D, the game utilises engaging mechanics and speech recognition technology, along with commonly used exercises, to provide speech therapy in an accessible and gamified format.

I thoroughly enjoyed working on this game, not only because it provided a great opportunity to implement new mechanics, such as a voice recognition feature and a card picking system, but also because I see significant potential in similar games and their benefits to society.

In this project, my prime focus was on implementing voice recognition system along with the card selection system. Silly sprites were also drawn by me (I know, I have great talent).
