Marintec X UTS Rapido Hackathon

❮ ❯

Imagine a scenario, where a crew member on a naval ship encounters one or multiple alarms on the primary engine. They lack immediate access to an expert or the required technical documentation. This system, which has ingested the required documentation and extracted the necessary insights, will provide a diagnosis and recommended actions.

I initially started this two-day hackathon without a team, but it turned out to be a huge success for me. My team and I secured first place at the Marinetec hackathon! It was an incredible experience filled with innovation, teamwork, and creativity.

Over the course of two intense days, we developed a system to help the crew on a naval ship diagnose and respond to alarms from the ship's primary engine. This experience not only demonstrated the power of teamwork and innovation but also provided an amazing opportunity to push the boundaries of what is possible.
