Procedurally Generated Dungeon in UE5

❮ ❯

Doom once revolutionized the genre and set a new standard for video game design.

Inspired by the quality of the original Doom, we have decided to recreate similar gameplay, but with a twist: our game will feature procedurally generated levels and multiplayer functionality, developed using Unreal Engine 5.

In this project, my prime focus was on creating procedurally generated interior of the level, post production effects and multiplayer integration.
