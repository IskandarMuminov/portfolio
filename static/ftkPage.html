For The King!

❮ ❯

The king has been overthrown, and your kingdom is in danger. Even though you're just a guard at a herb stall, all hope now rests on you!!!

I love to try new genres and practice my skills in something I haven't done before, so this is my attempt to create a point-click, top-down RPG.

In this game, my prime focus was on creating combat, enemy patrolling and RPG levelling systems.
